Read and listen to the Holy Quran on your device without limits or ads.

Quran App offers a clean, distraction-free environment to engage with the Quran, featuring:
- High-quality audio recitation with multiple renowned Qaris.
- Accurate prayer times based on your current location.
- comprehensive Azkar collection for daily supplications.
- Full offline caching, so you can read and listen even without an internet connection.
- 100% Free and Open Source (FOSS) with absolutely no tracking or advertisements.
